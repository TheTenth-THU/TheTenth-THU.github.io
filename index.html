About Me

CHEN Zhen-Xing (or spelled as Zhenxing Chen according to more general norms currently) is an undergraduate student in the Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University. Recently he is working on the research of Vision-and-Language Navigation (VLN).

Recent Projects

  • [Ongoing] Learning to Navigate in Open Urban Environments Using a Simple Sim2Real Strategy

    We have designed a unified autonomous navigation framework based on large models that seamlessly integrates indoor and outdoor navigation. This framework is initially trained in a virtual environment and then transferred to real-world scenarios through a sim2real approach. The proposed method has been rigorously tested and validated on Xiaomi's Cyberdog, demonstrating its reliability and effectiveness in real-world open city environment.

    The project is supported by FIBLAB, and supervised by Dr. Jie Feng.
